## What "Done" Looks Like

You can open a perpetual futures position, know your liquidation price before entering, monitor funding rate costs, assess a coin's on-chain valuation, and size positions so that a 40% overnight move doesn't wipe your account.

## Key Mindset Shift

**Volatility is not the enemy — leverage is.** Most crypto losses are not caused by being wrong about direction. They're caused by being too leveraged when the directional move happens on the wrong timeline. A 3x leveraged long Bitcoin position can be correct about the 6-month direction and still get liquidated by the 30% drawdown on the way there.

The on-chain tools exist because crypto markets are transparent in a way equity markets are not — you can see exchange inflows, long/short ratios, realized prices, and miner selling in real time. Learning to read this data is a genuine edge, but only if your position sizing means you can survive the noise.
