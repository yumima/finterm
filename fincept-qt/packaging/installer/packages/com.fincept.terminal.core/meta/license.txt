ABOUT THIS PROJECT
================================================================================

finterm is a community open-source fork — a local-first, offline-capable
financial-research terminal. There is no commercial entity behind it, no
SaaS account, no telemetry. Everything runs on your machine.

The project is distributed exclusively under AGPL-3.0-or-later. There is
no separate commercial license, no enterprise tier, no paid features.

If you redistribute or run a modified version as a network service, you
must comply with the AGPL's source-disclosure requirement (sections 6
and 13 of the license).
